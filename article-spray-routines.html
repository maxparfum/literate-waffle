Spray Routines for
Every Occasion

2025 Guide | 6 Routines
Back to Insights

How you apply a fragrance matters as much as which one you choose. The right routine changes everything — from how long it lasts to how people experience it around you.

🏋️ Active · Gym

The Gym Routine

One spray on your chest
One spray on the back of your neck
Pick something light and fresh — people are there to exercise, not to be enveloped in your trail
2 sprays · Light & fresh only
💥 Powerful · Beast Mode

Heavy Hitters

One spray on each side of the neck
One spray at the back of the neck
For strong fragrances, less is always more — let the fragrance do the work
3 sprays · Precision application
✨ Evening · Date Night

Date Night

One spray on each wrist
Two sprays around the collarbone
One spray at the back of the neck
A few additional sprays for projection — clothes, hair, the air in front of you
6–7 sprays · Full projection
⚡ Application · Weak Performers

Low-Longevity Frags

If a fragrance lasts under six hours, overspray intentionally
10–13 sprays is perfectly fine — it will fade quickly regardless
Layer on skin and clothes for best staying power
10–13 sprays · Embrace the fade
💼 Daytime · Office & School

Office & School

Left collarbone, right collarbone
Back of neck
One on each arm
Strong fragrances: scale back slightly — respect the shared space
5 sprays · Considerate projection
🌤️ Everyday · Casual

Casual Wear

Collarbones — left and right
Both arms
Back of neck
Balanced, safe, and appropriate for any errand or outing
5 sprays · Balanced & easy
✦
The Golden Rule

There's no rigid formula — the only real rule is to consider the people around you. Not everyone enjoys strong fragrance. Read the room, read the setting, and adjust accordingly.
